Know Thyself
Campaign, Branding, Digital
2025
00:00:00
"Know Thyself" is a reflective awareness campaign that explores how individuals often lose their sense of identity under pressure, suppressing their true desires, values, and individuality to meet external expectations. In a world driven by comparison, productivity, and social standards, the campaign highlights the growing disconnect between one's authentic self and the persona shaped for acceptance.

Drawing inspiration from anime quotes, the campaign uses these moments of raw, unfiltered truth—often expressed by characters free from real-world judgment—as emotional anchors. These quotes cut through societal noise, encouraging audiences to pause, reflect, and question who they are beneath expectations.

Set in Harajuku, the mock campaign leverages the district's bold culture of self-expression as a visual and thematic contrast to Tokyo's intense and often exhausting work culture. Through striking visuals, immersive installations, and introspective messaging, "Know Thyself" invites individuals to reclaim their identity and redefine their lives on their own terms.
