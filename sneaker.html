Reebok Club C 85: Atlanta 1996 Olympics
Inspired by the 1996 Atlanta Olympics and Reebok's role as a sponsor, this design incorporates linear patterns drawn from track and field lane markings. The flowing lines create a sense of motion, speed, and rhythm, reflecting the energy of athletic performance while capturing a bold, 90s sports aesthetic.
Movie collection item inspired from KANO 嘉農棒球隊 (2014)
When I was young this movie contributed to my passion as an athlete. The movie shows the journey of a small team in Chiayi climbing the ranks of high school baseball in the Japanese period. As underdogs they shocked fans nationwide as they continued to break records. I love how the movie described athletes as papaya fruits. Only at the brink of death are we able to surpass and produce sweet results.
