KÀN (台灣人ㄟ招牌) A compact 40-page publication exploring Taiwanese signage through bold color and assertive typography. Documenting signs from Taipei to Tainan, the design embraces high-contrast visuals, expressive layouts, capturing the raw energy of urban streetscapes. It examines the tension between visual chaos and cultural identity, exploring this attention-grabbing and controversially beautiful aesthetic.
